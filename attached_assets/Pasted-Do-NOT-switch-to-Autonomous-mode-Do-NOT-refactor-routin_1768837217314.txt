Do NOT switch to Autonomous mode.
Do NOT refactor routing or layout.
Do NOT add backend, validation, or submission logic.

This task is ONLY to IMPROVE the REGISTRATION UX
by collecting richer data in a calm, step-based flow.

----------------------------------
KEY UX RULE (VERY IMPORTANT)
----------------------------------
- DO NOT show numeric indicators like:
  “Step 1 of 6”
- Instead, show:
  - Clear step TITLES
  - Short descriptions of what data is being collected
- Each step should feel like a logical category,
  not a numbered task.

----------------------------------
GLOBAL REGISTRATION FLOW RULES
----------------------------------
- One step visible at a time
- No full-page scrolling
- Smooth animated transitions between steps
- Clear “Next” and “Back” actions
- Ability to exit and change role at any time

----------------------------------
STEP STRUCTURE (CATEGORY-BASED)
----------------------------------

🟣 STEP: BASIC PROFILE DETAILS
Description:
“Tell us who you are”

Fields (ALL ROLES):
- Full Name
- Email
- Country / Location (styled dropdown)
- Time Zone (dropdown)

----------------------------------

🟣 STEP: PROFESSIONAL IDENTITY
Description:
“Help us understand your background”

🧠 IDEA HOLDER:
- Primary interest area (multi-select)
- Problem domains you care about
- Have you previously worked on ideas? (Yes/No)

💻 DEVELOPER:
- Primary technical domain
- Secondary domains (optional)
- Current status:
  - Student
  - Working Professional
  - Freelancer
- Years of experience

💼 INVESTOR:
- Investor category:
  - Individual
  - Angel
  - VC
- Years of investing experience

----------------------------------

🟣 STEP: WORKING PREFERENCES
Description:
“How do you want to engage on DevConnect?”

🧠 IDEA HOLDER:
- What are you looking for?
  - Funding
  - Developers
  - Selling idea
- Preferred collaboration style

💻 DEVELOPER:
- Work preference:
  - Full-time
  - Part-time
  - Contract
- Availability (hours/week)
- Interested in equity-based work? (Yes/No)

💼 INVESTOR:
- Preferred involvement:
  - Passive
  - Advisory
  - Active
- Investment stage preference:
  - Idea
  - MVP
  - Scaling

----------------------------------

🟣 STEP: ORGANIZATION & AFFILIATION
Description:
“Optional professional associations”

🧠 IDEA HOLDER:
- Are you part of any community or startup group?

💻 DEVELOPER:
- Current / Previous company
- GitHub / Portfolio link (optional)

💼 INVESTOR:
- Do you represent an organization? (Yes/No)

If YES:
- Organization name
- Your role in the organization
- Organization type

----------------------------------

🟣 STEP: INTERESTS & GOALS
Description:
“What are you aiming to achieve?”

(ALL ROLES)
- Domains you are interested in
- Long-term goal on DevConnect
- What success looks like for you (short text)

----------------------------------

🟣 STEP: ABOUT YOU
Description:
“Introduce yourself to the community”

(ALL ROLES)
- Short bio (textarea)
- Optional tagline

----------------------------------
FINAL ACTION
----------------------------------

After the last step:
- Show a summary preview (read-only)
- Button:
  “Create Account as <Role Name>”
